This document demonstrates the Public Choice citation style.

\subsection{In-text citations}

Parenthetical citation: \pcite{Piano2018}.

Textual citation: \tcite{Becker1971} argues that market power enables discrimination.

Multiple citations: \pcite{Piano2018,Iannaccone1994,Becker2024}.

Citation with page number: \citep[p.~350]{Piano2018}.

\subsection{Various entry types}

A journal article: \pcite{Piano2018}.
A book: \pcite{Becker1971}.
A book chapter: \pcite{Scanzoni1986}.
A working paper: \pcite{Cinnirella2017}.
Multiple authors: \pcite{Becker2024}.
